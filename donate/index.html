Donate to the droidsec group!

Our research is limited by our resources. In an effort to improve this situation we are soliciting device and monetary donations from the community. All donations will be used to enable cutting edge security research on the Android platform.

Donate Devices

Every Android device is unique. Some security issues are device specific. Some issues affect a wide range of devices. Without a large test bed our testing abilities are limited. To donate a device, even an old one, contact: donate-device (dot) droidsec (at) qoop (dot) org.
