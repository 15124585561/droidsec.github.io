droidsec was founded in May 2012 in an effort advance the state of security of the Android platform. Group members include security researchers from many different companies in the mobile and security spaces. You can find most of the members on the #droidsec channel on the Freenode IRC network.

This web site serves to collect security relevant information published all across the Internet into one convienient location.

Join Us!

The group is currently open for participation from anyone interested. In particular, we are seeking motivated individuals to help push the site forward.

  • Web Designers Help us make the site look and feel better to visitors.
  • Security Researchers Execute on projects and showcase them on the site!
